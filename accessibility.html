Accessibility Statement

Last updated: October 13, 2025

Our commitment

GoldenAge Network is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users. We work to improve the user experience for everyone.

Standards

We follow industry best practices including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 where feasible.

Ongoing work

We regularly audit our site and update components to improve accessibility.

Contact

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us at accessibility@gasmp.net and we will respond within 5 business days.
